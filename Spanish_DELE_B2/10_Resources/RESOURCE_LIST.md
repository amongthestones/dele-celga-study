## Reading

### Newspapers (Spain)
elpais.com, elmundo.es, lavanguardia.com, abc.es

### Newspapers (Latin America)
eluniversal.com.mx, clarin.com, lanacion.com.ar, eltiempo.com

### Magazines
Muy Interesante, National Geographic España, Jot Down

### Accessible Literature
Short-story collections and contemporary fiction at B1–B2 level — useful authors to explore: Almudena Grandes, Rosa Montero, Juan José Millás, Isabel Allende, Mario Vargas Llosa. Graded readers from publishers like Difusión and Edinumen are a good bridge if full novels feel out of reach.

---

## Speaking Practice

### Language Exchange
Tandem, HelloTalk, ConversationExchange, italki Community.

### Paid Tutoring
italki, Preply, Verbling — search for DELE-trained tutors specifically; a few focused sessions on writing feedback are usually more valuable than weekly conversation classes.

---

## Writing Practice

### Feedback
- italki notebooks / community corrections
- LangCorrect
- HiNative (for quick questions)
- A DELE-trained tutor for essay corrections

### Model texts
Official DELE sample papers (CVC) and preparation books all include model answers — study a few before writing your own so you know what a strong answer looks like.
