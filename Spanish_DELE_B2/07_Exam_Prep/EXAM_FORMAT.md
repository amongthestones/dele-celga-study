# DELE B2 — Exam Format
## Estructura de la prueba

---

## Overview

The **DELE B2** (Diploma de Español como Lengua Extranjera, level B2) is issued by the **Instituto Cervantes**. It has four *pruebas* grouped into two **grupos de destrezas**, and you must pass **both groups**.

> ⚠️ **Confirm the current specification** at **examenes.cervantes.es** and study the official **modelos de examen** at the CVC (cvc.cervantes.es). Task counts and timings are updated periodically.

---

## The Four Pruebas

| Prueba | Skill | Time | Tasks | Items |
|--------|-------|------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Comprensión de lectura | 70 min | 4 | 36 |
| 2 | Comprensión auditiva | 40 min | 5 | 30 |
| 3 | Expresión e interacción escritas | 80 min | 2 | — |
| 4 | Expresión e interacción orales | ~15–20 min (+ ~20 min prep) | 3 | — |

### Grouping (you must pass BOTH groups)

- **Grupo 1 — Destrezas de lectura/escritura:** Prueba 1 (Lectura) + Prueba 3 (Escritura)
- **Grupo 2 — Destrezas orales:** Prueba 2 (Auditiva) + Prueba 4 (Oral)

Each group is scored out of 25; you need **≥ 60% in each group** (i.e. ~15/25). A strong score in one prueba can offset a weaker one **within the same group**, but you cannot offset across groups.

---

## Prueba 1 — Comprensión de lectura (70 min, 4 tasks, 36 items)

| Task | Format |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Multiple choice (3 options) on one longer text |
| 2 | Match statements/people to short texts |
| 3 | Insert removed sentences/fragments back into a text |
| 4 | Cloze: choose the correct word for each gap (3 options) |

→ Strategies in `03_Reading/MODULE_01_Reading_Strategies.md`.

## Prueba 2 — Comprensión auditiva (40 min, 5 tasks, 30 items)

Short messages, a conversation, an interview, matching monologues to statements, and a longer monologue. Audios are heard **twice**.

→ Strategies in `05_Listening/MODULE_01_Listening_Strategies.md`.

## Prueba 3 — Expresión e interacción escritas (80 min, 2 tasks)

- **Tarea 1:** a **formal letter** (~150–180 words) responding to a written/audio input.
- **Tarea 2:** choose **1 of 2** options — an opinion article/essay or a semi-formal text — based on a short stimulus (~150–180 words).

→ Guide + templates in `04_Writing/MODULE_01_Writing_Guide.md`; practice in `ESSAY_PROMPT_BANK.md`.

## Prueba 4 — Expresión e interacción orales (3 tasks, + prep time)

- **Tarea 1:** evaluate written proposals (monologue + conversation).
- **Tarea 2:** describe a photograph and discuss it.
- **Tarea 3:** role-play / imagined situation related to the photo.

→ Guide in `06_Speaking/MODULE_01_Speaking_Guide.md`.

---

## Assessment of the Productive Skills

Writing and speaking are rated against descriptors covering roughly: **task fulfilment, coherence/cohesion, range, and accuracy**. Address **every** point a task asks for — incomplete task fulfilment is the most common avoidable loss of marks.

---

## Registration & Logistics

- **Info & registration:** examenes.cervantes.es (exam dates, centres, fees by country).
- **Sample exams:** cvc.cervantes.es → DELE → modelos de examen.
- **Sittings:** several convocatorias per year; dates vary by level and centre.
- The full exam day runs ~3.5 hours including the oral and breaks.
