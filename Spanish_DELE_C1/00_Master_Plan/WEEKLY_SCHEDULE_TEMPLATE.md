## Tips for Maintaining Consistency

1. **Set a fixed study time** - Same time daily creates habit
2. **Prepare materials the night before** - Reduce friction
3. **Use the 2-minute rule** - If you don't feel like studying, commit to just 2 minutes
4. **Track your streak** - Visual motivation
5. **Find an accountability partner** - Someone to check in with
6. **Reward milestones** - Celebrate completing weeks/months
7. **Be flexible but committed** - Adjust schedule as needed, but don't skip days
