# Tutor Session Protocol
## How to run your weekly hour for maximum DELE writing progress

---

## The Problem This Solves

Your tutor hour was previously split across conversation, grammar, and reading — skills you can develop independently. Writing is the only skill where expert feedback is irreplaceable. This protocol redirects the hour entirely toward writing.

---

## Before Each Session

1. Write an essay draft using the next prompt from `ESSAY_PROMPT_BANK.md`
2. Save it as `04_Writing/drafts/essay_[number]_[date].md`
3. Send it to your tutor at least **24 hours before the session** if possible
4. Run the self-review checklist (Session 4 in `SESSION_CARDS.md`) before you send it — mark the parts you're unsure about

---

## During the Session (60 min)

### Part 1 — Tutor Feedback on Essay (25 min)

Ask your tutor to evaluate the essay using the four DELE rubric categories:

| Category | Question to ask |
|----------|----------------|
| Task completion | Did I address all the required points? What did I miss? |
| Coherence/cohesion | Are my paragraph transitions logical? Is any section hard to follow? |
| Linguistic range | Where am I repeating myself or using B2-level vocabulary when C1 exists? |
| Accuracy | Which grammar errors are C1-critical (not just minor slips)? |

Take notes. Write the specific corrections in the essay file after the session.

### Part 2 — Targeted Drilling (25 min)

Based on the feedback from Part 1, pick **one specific issue** and drill it:
- If the error was subjunctive: tutor gives 5 examples → you reformulate → tutor corrects
- If the error was connector use: tutor gives a paragraph → you rewrite with better connectors
- If the error was register: tutor identifies colloquialisms → you replace with formal alternatives

### Part 3 — Next Prompt Assignment (10 min)

- Tutor assigns a writing focus for next week's essay (e.g., "this time emphasize impersonal constructions", "practice the report format")
- You note which prompt number you'll write next (the next in sequence from `ESSAY_PROMPT_BANK.md`)
- Optional: tutor recommends one grammar point to review before next session

---

## After Each Session (within 24 hours)

1. Rewrite the weakest paragraph of your essay incorporating feedback
2. Add any new phrases or structures your tutor highlighted to `THEMATIC_VOCABULARY.md` under the relevant theme
3. Update your essays log in `SESSION_CARDS.md` with the tutor's score and key feedback

---

## What to Do If You Didn't Write an Essay Before the Session

Use the hour differently:
1. Complete one writing task from *El Cronómetro C1* during the session under timed conditions
2. Tutor marks it immediately
3. Spend remaining time on the specific error from Part 2 above

Do not use the hour for general conversation or grammar unless you have already written that week.

---

## Tracking Tutor Progress

After every 4 sessions, look at your essays log and ask:
- Are the same errors repeating?
- Has my self-score improved?
- Which text type do I still avoid?

Share this review with your tutor to reorient focus if needed.
