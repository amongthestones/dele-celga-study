---

## What is DELE C1?

The DELE C1 certifies that you can:
- Understand a wide range of demanding, longer texts and recognize implicit meaning
- Express yourself fluently and spontaneously without much obvious searching for expressions
- Use language flexibly and effectively for social, academic, and professional purposes
- Produce clear, well-structured, detailed text on complex subjects

---

## DELE C1 Exam Structure

| Section | Duration | Tasks | Weight |
|---------|----------|-------|--------|
| **Reading Comprehension** | 90 min | 5 tasks (40 items) | 25% |
| **Listening Comprehension** | 50 min | 4 tasks (30 items) | 25% |
| **Written Expression** | 80 min | 2 tasks | 25% |
| **Oral Expression** | 20 min + 20 min prep | 3 tasks | 25% |

**Passing Score:** Minimum 60% in each group:
- Group 1: Reading + Writing (combined minimum 60%)
- Group 2: Listening + Speaking (combined minimum 60%)

## Daily Habits (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Vocabulary Review** (15 min)
   - Review flashcards (Anki recommended)
   - Learn 10-15 new words

2. **Active Listening** (30 min)
   - Podcast, news, or audiobook
   - Mix of focused and passive listening

3. **Reading** (20 min)
   - News articles, novels, or academic texts
   - Note new vocabulary and expressions

4. **Thinking in Spanish** (throughout the day)
   - Narrate your actions mentally
   - Plan your day in Spanish

## Skill-Specific Strategies

### Reading (Comprensión de Lectura)

**C1 Reading Requirements:**
- Understand long, complex texts
- Identify implicit meaning and tone
- Follow complex arguments
- Understand specialized articles

**Practice Strategies:**
1. Read diverse text types daily
2. Practice skimming and scanning
3. Work on vocabulary in context
4. Analyze text structure
5. Time your reading exercises

### Listening (Comprensión Auditiva)

**C1 Listening Requirements:**
- Understand extended speech
- Follow complex arguments
- Understand most TV and films
- Grasp implicit attitudes and relationships

**Practice Strategies:**
1. Listen to a variety of accents
2. Practice with authentic materials
3. Work on note-taking skills
4. Do prediction exercises
5. Gradually remove subtitles

### Writing (Expresión Escrita)

**C1 Writing Requirements:**
- Write clear, well-structured texts
- Express viewpoints at length
- Write about complex subjects
- Use appropriate style and tone

**Text Types for DELE C1:**
- Formal letters and emails
- Opinion essays
- Reviews and reports
- Articles and blog posts

**Practice Strategies:**
1. Write regularly (minimum 2x/week)
2. Get feedback from natives/teachers
3. Study model texts
4. Build a bank of useful expressions
5. Practice within time limits

### Speaking (Expresión Oral)

**C1 Speaking Requirements:**
- Express yourself fluently
- Use language flexibly
- Present clear descriptions
- Develop arguments systematically

**DELE C1 Speaking Tasks:**
1. Presentation based on a text and graph
2. Discussion with examiner
3. Negotiation task with another candidate

**Practice Strategies:**
1. Practice monologues on varied topics
2. Record and self-evaluate
3. Work with tutors/language partners
4. Develop opinions on current events
5. Practice turn-taking and negotiation
