## Phase 1: Writing Foundation (Apr–Sep 2026)

**Goal:** Write consistently at C1 standard before introducing exam technique.

**Session rotation (see SESSION_CARDS.md):**
- 2 × Writing sessions per week
- 1 × Vocabulary session
- 1 × Listening session
- 1 × Reading session
- Tutor hour: 100% writing (see TUTOR_SESSION_PROTOCOL.md)

**Milestones:**
- [ ] Buy *El Cronómetro C1* (Edinumen) — buy in the first two weeks
- [ ] Complete diagnostic test (`07_Exam_Prep/DIAGNOSTIC_TEST.md`)
- [ ] Write 20+ essays by end of Phase 1
- [ ] Identify your 3 most common writing errors by month 3
- [ ] Check DELE exam registration deadline for your target session

---

## Phase 2: Integrated Practice (Oct 2026–Mar 2027)

**Goal:** Sharpen exam technique in all four skills. Writing continues as priority.

**What changes in Phase 2:**
- El Cronómetro C1 becomes your primary resource for reading and listening tasks
- Add timed conditions to all practice (this is new — Phase 1 is untimed)
- One practice exam section per month (timed, scored, reviewed)
- Continue 2 writing sessions per week

**Milestones:**
- [ ] Complete 4+ timed reading sections (El Cronómetro C1)
- [ ] Write 20+ more essays across all text types (letter, report, opinion, article)
- [ ] Achieve 70%+ on practice reading tasks consistently
- [ ] Complete 2 full timed exam sections

---

## Phase 3: Exam Technique (Apr–Aug 2027)

**Goal:** Full mock exams, final gap targeting, exam logistics.

**What changes in Phase 3:**
- Saturday session = full mock exam section (no exceptions)
- Writing review: compare your essays to model answers in El Cronómetro
- Reduce vocabulary sessions — consolidate what you have
- Speaking: practice the 3 oral tasks (presentation, discussion, negotiation)

**Milestones:**
- [ ] 4+ full mock exams completed
- [ ] Exam registered and confirmed (Santiago or Vigo exam center)
- [ ] Top 3 weak areas identified and addressed
- [ ] Oral tasks practiced with tutor or language partner
