# Essay Prompt Bank — DELE C1
## 60 Practice Prompts | Work through in order, starting at #1

**How to use:** In Session 1 (Writing A), open this file and find the next prompt you haven't written. Do that one. Don't choose — just do the next one in sequence.

**Mark each prompt when done:**
- [ ] = not yet written
- [x] = draft complete
- [r] = reviewed with tutor

## PROMPTS 1–15: Formal Letters (FL)

### 1. [ ] Queja sobre transporte público
**Text type:** Formal complaint letter | **Words:** 200–230

You are a resident of a Galician city. The local bus service has recently reduced routes, making it difficult to access the hospital, and the remaining routes are frequently late. Write a formal letter to the municipal transport authority expressing your concerns and requesting specific improvements. Address:
- The specific impact on residents (especially elderly and those without cars)
- Your request for the routes to be restored
- A proposal for a better complaints system

---

### 2. [ ] Solicitud de beca de investigación
**Text type:** Formal request letter | **Words:** 200–230

You are a Spanish language student who has discovered a grant for language and culture research projects. Write a formal letter to the grants committee requesting funding to develop educational materials about regional languages in Spain. Address:
- Your background and qualifications
- The specific project you would develop
- Why this project deserves funding over others

---

### 3. [ ] Propuesta a una empresa cultural
**Text type:** Formal proposal letter | **Words:** 200–230

You work at a cultural association in Galicia. A regional newspaper has announced it is cutting its arts and culture section to reduce costs. Write a formal letter to the editor proposing an alternative model (e.g., community contributions, digital-only format, partnerships). Address:
- Why cultural journalism matters in the region
- Your specific alternative proposal
- What you ask the newspaper to do

---

### 4. [ ] Reclamación a una empresa de servicios digitales
**Text type:** Formal complaint letter | **Words:** 200–230

You signed up for an online language learning platform. After paying for an annual subscription, access to your account was blocked due to a technical error and the customer service response has been inadequate. Write a formal letter demanding resolution. Address:
- The timeline of events and attempts to resolve the issue
- The financial and practical impact
- What resolution you expect and by when

---

### 5. [ ] Carta a la dirección de un hospital
**Text type:** Formal letter of concern | **Words:** 200–230

You have recently been a patient (or accompanied a family member) in a public hospital and observed understaffing issues that affected the quality of care. Write a formal letter to the hospital director. Address:
- Specific observations (without identifying individuals)
- The systemic issue you believe is causing the problem
- Concrete recommendations for improvement

---

### 6. [ ] Respuesta a una convocatoria de empleo
**Text type:** Formal application letter | **Words:** 200–230

A cultural organization is looking for a project coordinator to develop bilingual (Spanish/Galician) educational programs. Write a formal application letter. Address:
- Your relevant experience and skills
- Why you are interested in this specific role
- What you would bring to the team

---

### 7. [ ] Propuesta de proyecto a un ayuntamiento
**Text type:** Formal proposal letter | **Words:** 200–230

Your neighborhood lacks green spaces and a local residents' association wants to propose converting an abandoned plot of land into a community garden. Write a formal letter to the town hall. Address:
- The current problem and its impact on residents
- Your specific proposal with practical details
- How the town hall could support the initiative

---

### 8. [ ] Queja sobre publicidad engañosa
**Text type:** Formal complaint letter | **Words:** 200–230

You purchased a product after seeing an advertisement that made claims the product does not fulfill. You have already contacted the company without result. Write a formal complaint to the consumer protection agency. Address:
- The specific misleading claims in the advertisement
- Your unsuccessful attempt to resolve it with the company
- What action you expect from the agency

---

### 9. [ ] Carta a una editorial
**Text type:** Formal letter | **Words:** 200–230

You are a writer who has completed a collection of short stories. Write a formal letter to a Spanish literary publisher submitting your work for consideration. Address:
- A brief, compelling description of the collection
- Your target readership
- Why this publisher specifically is the right fit

---

### 10. [ ] Propuesta de colaboración entre instituciones
**Text type:** Formal proposal letter | **Words:** 200–230

You represent a language school that wants to propose a formal collaboration with a local university's Spanish department. Write a letter to the department head. Address:
- What the collaboration would involve
- The benefit to students of both institutions
- The first concrete step you propose

---

### 11. [ ] Carta de apoyo a una causa cultural
**Text type:** Formal letter of support | **Words:** 200–230

A community library in a small town is being closed due to budget cuts. You are writing as a private citizen to the regional culture department to oppose the closure. Address:
- The role the library plays in the community
- The disproportionate impact on vulnerable groups
- What alternatives to closure you suggest

---

### 12. [ ] Queja sobre condiciones laborales
**Text type:** Formal complaint letter | **Words:** 200–230

You are an employee who has experienced repeated violations of agreed working conditions (e.g., unpaid overtime, changes to contract terms without consent). You have raised the issue internally without result. Write a formal complaint to the labor inspectorate. Address:
- A factual account of the violations
- The impact on you and colleagues
- What outcome you are requesting

---

### 13. [ ] Solicitud de información sobre un programa público
**Text type:** Formal request letter | **Words:** 200–230

You have read about a government program that subsidizes energy efficiency renovations for homes, but the information on the official website is unclear and contradictory. Write a formal letter to the responsible ministry requesting clarification. Address:
- Specific questions you need answered
- The purpose for which you need the information
- A request for a response within a reasonable timeframe

---

### 14. [ ] Propuesta para un programa de radio
**Text type:** Formal proposal letter | **Words:** 200–230

A public radio station has announced it is accepting proposals for new cultural programming. Write a formal letter pitching an original program concept. Address:
- The concept and format of the program
- Why it meets a gap in current programming
- Your credentials or team to produce it

---

### 15. [ ] Carta de respuesta a una organización
**Text type:** Formal response letter | **Words:** 200–230

A professional association has sent you a letter asking you to justify why you missed a mandatory meeting that had significant administrative consequences. Write a formal response explaining the circumstances and proposing a resolution. Address:
- A clear, factual explanation (circumstances beyond your control)
- An acknowledgment of the impact
- Your proposed next steps

## PROMPTS 16–30: Opinion Essays & Articles (OE / AR)

### 16. [ ] La inteligencia artificial en la educación
**Text type:** Opinion article for a cultural magazine | **Words:** 200–230

*"La inteligencia artificial en las aulas es una amenaza para el pensamiento crítico."*

Write an opinion article responding to this statement. You may agree, disagree, or present a nuanced position. Your article should present at least two developed arguments and reach a clear conclusion.

---

### 17. [ ] El trabajo en remoto y la calidad de vida
**Text type:** Opinion essay | **Words:** 200–230

Write an opinion essay on whether remote work has genuinely improved quality of life for workers, or whether it has created new forms of exploitation and isolation. Present a clear position with supporting arguments.

---

### 18. [ ] La gentrificación de las ciudades
**Text type:** Magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article for an urban affairs publication examining the gentrification of city centers. Consider: who benefits, who is displaced, and what — if any — policy responses are effective.

---

### 19. [ ] Las redes sociales y la salud mental juvenil
**Text type:** Opinion article | **Words:** 200–230

*"Las plataformas digitales son las principales responsables de la crisis de salud mental entre los jóvenes."*

Respond to this statement with a developed opinion article. Consider multiple angles: platform design, parental responsibility, educational systems, individual agency.

---

### 20. [ ] El valor del multilingüismo en Europa
**Text type:** Opinion essay | **Words:** 200–230

Write an opinion essay arguing for or against the idea that the European Union invests too little in promoting multilingualism among its citizens. Use concrete examples.

---

### 21. [ ] La economía circular: ¿solución real o moda?
**Text type:** Magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article for an environmental magazine analyzing whether the "circular economy" model represents a genuine shift in how we produce and consume, or whether it is primarily a marketing tool.

---

### 22. [ ] El papel de los medios públicos
**Text type:** Opinion essay | **Words:** 200–230

In an era of streaming services and social media, is public broadcasting still necessary? Write an opinion essay defending your position with developed arguments.

---

### 23. [ ] Turismo masivo y patrimonio cultural
**Text type:** Magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article examining the tension between mass tourism and the preservation of cultural heritage in cities like Barcelona, Venice, or Santiago de Compostela. What models of regulation, if any, work?

---

### 24. [ ] El debate sobre la semana laboral de cuatro días
**Text type:** Opinion article | **Words:** 200–230

Several countries have trialed a four-day working week with mixed results. Write an opinion article either defending or critiquing the model, using evidence from those trials.

---

### 25. [ ] Lenguas regionales: ¿protección o imposición?
**Text type:** Opinion essay | **Words:** 200–230

Some argue that regional language policies (mandatory use in schools, public services) protect cultural identity. Others argue they impose a language on citizens who don't want it. Write a nuanced opinion essay.

---

### 26. [ ] La meritocracia como mito
**Text type:** Magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

*"El éxito es el resultado del esfuerzo individual."* Write an article examining whether meritocracy is a genuine principle or a social myth, and what the implications are for policy.

---

### 27. [ ] El envejecimiento de la población y el Estado del bienestar
**Text type:** Opinion essay | **Words:** 200–230

Spain has one of the world's fastest-ageing populations. Write an opinion essay on whether the current welfare state model is sustainable and what changes — if any — are necessary.

---

### 28. [ ] La cultura de la cancelación
**Text type:** Opinion article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an opinion article examining "cancel culture": whether it represents legitimate social accountability or a form of censorship that harms public discourse.

---

### 29. [ ] Alimentación y responsabilidad social
**Text type:** Magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article examining whether individual dietary choices (e.g., reducing meat consumption) can make a meaningful difference to the environment, or whether systemic change is the only effective lever.

---

### 30. [ ] El papel de la literatura en la sociedad contemporánea
**Text type:** Opinion essay | **Words:** 200–230

In a world of podcasts, short videos, and social media, does literature still play an irreplaceable role? Write an opinion essay defending your position.

## PROMPTS 31–45: Reports (IN)

### 31. [ ] Informe sobre el uso de dispositivos digitales en el trabajo
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

You work for a human resources consultancy. Write a report for a client company based on the following findings from an internal survey:
- 73% of employees check work email outside working hours
- 58% report difficulty disconnecting
- 34% have requested changes to digital communication policies

Address: introduction/objective, key findings, conclusions, recommendations.

---

### 32. [ ] Informe sobre hábitos de lectura entre jóvenes
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

You are writing for a cultural foundation. Based on a study showing that reading for pleasure has declined 40% among 15–25 year olds over the past decade (but audiobook and podcast consumption has tripled), write a report. Address: findings, implications for literacy programs, recommendations.

---

### 33. [ ] Informe sobre transporte sostenible en una ciudad mediana
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A regional government has commissioned a report on the viability of expanding cycling infrastructure in a medium-sized city where currently only 3% of journeys are made by bicycle (vs. 22% by car). Write the report. Address: current situation, barriers to change, recommendations with a proposed pilot.

---

### 34. [ ] Informe sobre bienestar de empleados en una empresa
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

Following an anonymous staff survey at a 150-person company, you must report to management on the results:
- 61% feel their workload has increased significantly in the last year
- 44% say they lack recognition for their work
- 29% are considering leaving within 12 months

Address: key findings, root causes identified in comments, priority recommendations.

---

### 35. [ ] Informe sobre el turismo en una localidad gallega
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A Galician municipality has asked you to assess the impact of increased tourism (the town saw a 180% increase in visitors over five years following its appearance on a popular travel program). Address: economic benefits, cultural and environmental pressures, recommendations for sustainable tourism management.

---

### 36. [ ] Informe sobre voluntariado universitario
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A Spanish university wants to understand why its volunteer program participation dropped from 800 students per year to 310 in three years. You have conducted focus groups. Write a report identifying the main barriers and recommending how to reinvigorate the program.

---

### 37. [ ] Informe sobre alimentación escolar
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A regional education department has asked for a report on the nutritional quality of school canteen menus following a study showing that 38% of menus do not meet official nutritional guidelines. Address: scope of the problem, contributing factors, proposed guidelines for improvement.

---

### 38. [ ] Informe sobre el acceso a la cultura en zonas rurales
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A cultural foundation is concerned that residents of rural areas in Spain have significantly fewer cultural offerings than urban residents (libraries, theaters, exhibitions). Write a report on the scale of the gap and propose three concrete measures to reduce it.

---

### 39. [ ] Informe sobre el uso de espacios públicos post-pandemia
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

You have been asked to assess how the use of urban public spaces has changed in a Spanish city following the pandemic (increase in outdoor activities, emergence of informal markets, new conflicts between uses). Address findings and recommend how the city council should respond.

---

### 40. [ ] Informe sobre programas de mentoring laboral
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A professional association has run a six-month mentoring program pairing senior professionals with recent graduates. Participation was high but 40% of pairs stopped meeting after month 3. Write a report evaluating the program and recommending improvements.

---

### 41. [ ] Informe sobre el abandono escolar temprano
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

Spain has one of the highest early school-leaving rates in the EU. Write a report for an education ministry examining the key factors (economic, family, motivational) and proposing targeted interventions.

---

### 42. [ ] Informe sobre salud digital y menores
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A children's welfare organization has asked you to assess the evidence on the impact of smartphones on children under 12, considering both risks (sleep disruption, content exposure) and benefits (educational use, family communication). Write a balanced report with recommendations.

---

### 43. [ ] Informe sobre movilidad internacional de estudiantes
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A European university network wants to understand why participation in international exchange programs has declined post-pandemic. Write a report identifying barriers (financial, linguistic, personal) and recommending how to make mobility more accessible.

---

### 44. [ ] Informe sobre el envejecimiento en el entorno rural
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

Many small Spanish municipalities are losing young residents and aging rapidly. Write a report for a regional government examining the causes and consequences of this demographic shift, and recommending two or three targeted interventions.

---

### 45. [ ] Informe sobre consumo cultural digital
**Text type:** Report | **Words:** 200–230

A cultural ministry has commissioned a report on how streaming platforms have changed cultural consumption habits in Spain over the past five years. Address: growth in consumption, shift away from traditional media, implications for funding and regulation of cultural content.

---

## PROMPTS 46–55: Reviews (RE)

### 46. [ ] Reseña de una novela reciente
**Text type:** Review for a literary magazine | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a Spanish-language novel you have recently read. Your review should cover: brief summary (without spoilers), narrative structure and style, themes, strengths and weaknesses, and recommendation to which type of reader.

*(Use a real novel you have read or are reading. If you haven't read one recently, use: "La ciudad y los perros" — Mario Vargas Llosa, or "Soldados de Salamina" — Javier Cercas.)*

---

### 47. [ ] Reseña de una película
**Text type:** Film review for a cultural supplement | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a Spanish-language film (or a film with Spanish-language themes). Cover: director and context, plot summary, cinematography/acting, themes, your overall assessment.

---

### 48. [ ] Reseña de una exposición de arte
**Text type:** Review for a cultural magazine | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of an art exhibition (real or imagined). Cover: the artist or theme, what the exhibition contained, what worked and what didn't, and for whom you would recommend it.

---

### 49. [ ] Reseña de un ensayo o libro de no ficción
**Text type:** Review for an intellectual magazine | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a non-fiction book (essay, biography, journalism) in Spanish or translated into Spanish. Cover: the author's argument, the evidence used, the quality of the writing, and your assessment of its contribution.

---

### 50. [ ] Reseña de un espectáculo teatral o musical
**Text type:** Review for a performing arts publication | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a live performance (theater, concert, dance) you have attended. Cover: the work performed, the quality of the performance, the audience experience, and your overall assessment.

---

### 51. [ ] Reseña de un podcast o programa de radio
**Text type:** Review for a media magazine | **Words:** 180–220

Write a critical review of a Spanish-language podcast. Cover: the format and concept, the quality of presentation, the content depth, who the target audience is, and whether it achieves its stated purpose.

---

### 52. [ ] Reseña de un videojuego con narrativa compleja
**Text type:** Review for a culture and technology publication | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a video game with significant narrative or cultural content (e.g., a game dealing with historical events, moral dilemmas, or social themes). Assess it both as a cultural product and as a game.

---

### 53. [ ] Reseña de un documental
**Text type:** Review for a documentary film magazine | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a documentary (on any subject). Cover: the subject and approach, the evidence and sources used, the filmmaker's point of view, and whether it succeeds in what it sets out to do.

---

### 54. [ ] Reseña de un festival cultural
**Text type:** Review for a travel and culture magazine | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a cultural festival (music, literature, cinema, gastronomy). Cover: what the festival offers, the atmosphere and organization, highlights, and whether you would recommend it.

---

### 55. [ ] Reseña de una aplicación educativa
**Text type:** Review for an education technology publication | **Words:** 180–220

Write a review of a language learning or educational app. Cover: the learning approach it uses, the quality and variety of content, usability, and whether it delivers on its promises.

---

## PROMPTS 56–60: Mixed / Integrated Tasks (AR)

### 56. [ ] Artículo sobre los beneficios del bilingüismo
**Text type:** Article for a parenting magazine | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article aimed at parents considering raising children bilingually. Cover: the cognitive and social benefits of bilingualism, common fears or misconceptions, and practical advice for bilingual parenting.

---

### 57. [ ] Artículo de divulgación científica
**Text type:** Science communication article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article explaining a complex scientific or social concept to a general educated audience. Choose a topic you know well (e.g., how memory works, the science of sleep, the economics of inequality). Focus on clarity and accuracy without oversimplifying.

---

### 58. [ ] Artículo sobre identidad regional y lengua
**Text type:** Cultural magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article exploring the relationship between regional language (e.g., Galician, Catalan, Basque) and personal or collective identity. This is a topic you have direct experience with — draw on it.

---

### 59. [ ] Artículo de opinión sobre la vivienda
**Text type:** Opinion article for a newspaper | **Words:** 200–230

Housing affordability has become a defining political issue in Spain. Write an opinion article presenting your view on whether the problem is fundamentally one of supply, demand, speculation, or policy failure — and what should be done.

---

### 60. [ ] Artículo de cierre de año
**Text type:** Year-end magazine article | **Words:** 200–230

Write an article reflecting on a significant cultural, social, or political development of the past year and what it reveals about contemporary society. Choose something you followed closely and have a genuine view on.
