## RECOMMENDED SCHEDULE

### Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Foundation
- Focus on grammar textbook
- Daily news reading
- Podcast listening (30 min/day)
- Vocabulary building with Anki

### Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Skills Development
- Move to DELE-specific materials
- Increase reading complexity
- Start writing practice
- Find speaking partner

### Phase 3 (Months 13-18): Exam Preparation
- Use El Cronómetro
- Timed practice exams
- Focus on weak areas
- Regular speaking practice

### Phase 4 (Months 19-24): Final Preparation
- Full mock exams
- Review all materials
- Fine-tune strategies
- Maintain confidence
