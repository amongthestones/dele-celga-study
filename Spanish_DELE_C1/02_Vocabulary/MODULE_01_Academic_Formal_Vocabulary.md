# Module 1: Academic and Formal Vocabulary
## Essential C1 Vocabulary for Academic and Professional Contexts

---

## Overview

C1 requires sophisticated vocabulary for discussing complex topics in academic and professional settings. This module covers essential formal vocabulary.

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: Word Formation

Form nouns from these verbs:
1. analizar → el _______________
2. desarrollar → el _______________
3. investigar → la _______________
4. transformar → la _______________
5. evaluar → la _______________

### Exercise 2: Collocations

Match the verbs with their objects:
1. sacar a) medidas
2. adoptar b) conclusiones
3. desempeñar c) un acuerdo
4. alcanzar d) un papel
5. llegar a e) un objetivo

### Exercise 3: Context Usage

Complete with appropriate vocabulary:
1. Es necesario _______ (take measures) urgentes.
2. El estudio _______ (raises) cuestiones importantes.
3. _______ (In the context of) la crisis, debemos actuar.
4. Hay que _______ (take into account) todos los factores.
5. Esta política _______ (entails) ciertos riesgos.

---

## Answer Key

### Exercise 1:
1. el análisis
2. el desarrollo
3. la investigación
4. la transformación
5. la evaluación

### Exercise 2:
1-b, 2-a, 3-d, 4-e, 5-c

### Exercise 3:
1. adoptar medidas
2. plantea
3. En el contexto de
4. tener en cuenta
5. conlleva

## Study Tips

1. **Create themed vocabulary cards** organized by topic
2. **Learn collocations** not just individual words
3. **Read academic articles** and note new vocabulary in context
4. **Practice using formal vocabulary** in your writing
5. **Record yourself** using these words in sentences
