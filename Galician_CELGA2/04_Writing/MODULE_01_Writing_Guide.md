# Module 1: Writing Guide for CELGA 2
## Expresión escrita

---

## Overview

CELGA 2 writing asks for one or two **short texts (~150–200 words)** in everyday formats: an email or letter (formal or informal), a note/message, or a short informative/narrative/opinion text. You are judged on completing the task, writing correctly, and writing **like Galician, not Spanish**.

> Confirm current word counts and task types in the sample exams at **lingua.gal**.

---

## Part 1: What Examiners Reward

| Criterion | What it means here |
|-----------|--------------------|
| **Adecuación** | You do what's asked, in the right register |
| **Corrección** | Grammar and, crucially, **contractions, clitic placement, verb forms, no castelanismos** |
| **Riqueza** | A range of structures and vocabulary, not just *e… e… pero* |
| **Cohesión** | Linked ideas, paragraphs, connectors |

> The fastest way to lose marks as a Spanish speaker is **interference**: writing *he comido* (use *comín*), *Te escribo* (use *Escríboche*), *para o* contracted wrongly, *muy* instead of *moi*. Self-check for these every time.

## Part 2: Text Types and How to Build Them

### Correo / carta formal (formal email or letter)

```
Estimado/a Sr./Sra.: (or: Bos días:)

[Por que escribo]  — Póñome en contacto con vostede para…

[Corpo]            — develop each point the task asks for, one idea per paragraph

[Peche]            — Quedo á espera da súa resposta. / Grazas de antemán.

Atentamente,
[Nome]
```

**Útil:** *Póñome en contacto para…* · *Gustaríame solicitar…* · *Agradézolle…* · *En relación con…* · *Quedo á espera de…*

### Correo / mensaxe informal (to a friend or family)

```
Ola, [nome]:

[Saúdo + motivo]   — Que tal? Escríboche para contarche…

[Corpo]            — natural, friendly; use enclitic pronouns (cóntoche, vinte…)

[Peche]            — Xa me dis algo. Apertas, / Unha aperta,
[Nome]
```

**Útil:** *Que tal estás?* · *Escríboche para…* · *A ver se quedamos…* · *Xa me dis algo.* · *Apertas / Bicos.*

### Texto informativo / narrativo

Open with the topic, develop in 2–3 short paragraphs, close with a brief conclusion or opinion. Use past tenses correctly for narration (**pretérito** for events, **imperfecto** for background) — remember Galician has **no compound past**, so *cheguei*, not *hei chegado*.

---

## Part 3: Connectors (use a range)

| Function | Galician |
|----------|----------|
| Add | e, tamén, ademais, asemade |
| Contrast | pero, mais, porén, aínda que, no entanto |
| Cause | porque, xa que, dado que, posto que |
| Consequence | por iso, polo tanto, así que, daquela |
| Sequence | primeiro, despois, a seguir, por último, finalmente |
| Opinion | na miña opinión, ao meu ver, coido que, paréceme que |
| Conclusion | en resumo, en definitiva, para rematar |

---

## Part 4: A Pre-submission Checklist

- [ ] Did I cover **every** point the task asked for?
- [ ] Right **register** (vostede vs ti) and kept consistent?
- [ ] **Contractions** correct (do, da, no, na, polo, nun, cunha…)?
- [ ] **Pronoun placement**: enclitic by default (*escríboche*), proclitic after *non/que/xa/* question words?
- [ ] No **compound tenses** (*comín*, not *hei comido*)?
- [ ] No **castelanismos** (moi not muy; entón not entonces; grazas not gracias)?
- [ ] Possessives with **article** (*o meu*, *a miña*)?
- [ ] Within the **word count**?

## Part 5: Worked Mini-example (informal email, ~70 words)

> Ola, Marta:
>
> Que tal estás? Escríboche para convidarte á miña festa de aniversario o sábado que vén na miña casa. Imos cear sobre as nove e despois poñemos música. Se queres, podes traer a túa irmá. Avísame se podes vir, que teño que mercar a comida. Xa me dis algo!
>
> Unha aperta,
> Antía

Notice: enclitic pronouns (*escríboche*, *convidarte*, *avísame*), contractions (*á*, *na*), article + possessive (*a miña casa*, *a túa irmá*), no compound tenses.

## Exercises

### Exercise 1: De-interfere
Rewrite in correct Galician:
1. *Te escribo para invitarte.*
2. *He terminado el trabajo.*
3. *Muy gracias por todo.*

### Exercise 2: Write
Write a ~150-word **informal email** to a friend inviting them to spend a weekend in your town. Mention: when, what you'll do, and what they should bring. (See the prompt bank for more tasks.)
