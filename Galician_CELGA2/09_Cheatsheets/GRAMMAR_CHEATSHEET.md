## CONNECTORS

### Addition
- e (and)
- tamén (also)
- ademais (besides)

### Contrast
- pero (but)
- porén (however)
- aínda que (although)

### Cause
- porque (because)
- xa que (since)

### Consequence
- por iso (therefore)
- así que (so)

### Time
- cando (when)
- mentres (while)
- despois (after)
- antes (before)

---

## SER vs ESTAR

**SER for:**
- Identity/profession
- Origin
- Characteristics
- Time
- Events

**ESTAR for:**
- Location
- Temporary states
- Feelings
- Progressive (estar + gerund)
