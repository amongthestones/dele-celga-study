## What is CELGA?

CELGA (Certificado de Lingua Galega) is the official certification system for Galician language proficiency, administered by the Xunta de Galicia through the Secretaría Xeral de Política Lingüística.

### CELGA Levels
| Level | CEFR Equivalent | Description |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| CELGA 1 | A2 | Basic user |
| CELGA 2 | B1 | Independent user (threshold) |
| CELGA 3 | B2 | Independent user (vantage) |
| CELGA 4 | C1 | Proficient user |
| CELGA 5 | C2 | Proficient user (mastery) |

---

## CELGA 2 Exam Structure

| Section | Description | Duration |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Comprensión oral** | Listening comprehension | ~30 min |
| **Comprensión escrita** | Reading comprehension | ~45 min |
| **Expresión escrita** | Written expression | ~45 min |
| **Expresión oral** | Speaking (individual + interaction) | ~15 min |

**Passing requirement:** Minimum 50% in each section, 60% overall

## Why Galician?

Galician (Galego) is:
- A Romance language spoken by ~2.5 million people
- Official language of Galicia (with Spanish)
- Closely related to Portuguese
- Rich literary and cultural tradition
- Your Spanish knowledge will help significantly!

### Your Advantage: Spanish Speaker

As a B2 Spanish speaker, you have a significant advantage:
- Shared vocabulary (~85% cognates)
- Similar grammar structures
- Some shared verb conjugations
- Familiar writing system

**Key differences to focus on:**
- Different vowel system (7 vowels vs Spanish's 5)
- Article contractions (ao, do, no, polo, etc.)
- Different verb endings
- Unique vocabulary items
- Pronunciation differences

## Galician vs Spanish: Key Differences

### Articles & Contractions
| Spanish | Galician | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| el | o | o libro |
| la | a | a casa |
| los | os | os homes |
| las | as | as mulleres |
| al | ao | vou ao cine |
| del | do | a casa do veciño |
| en el | no | está no coche |
| por el | polo | vai polo camiño |

### Spelling Differences
| Spanish | Galician | Meaning |
|---------|----------|---------|
| noche | noite | night |
| leche | leite | milk |
| mucho | moito | much |
| ocho | oito | eight |
| hijo | fillo | son |
| ojo | ollo | eye |
| pueblo | pobo | village |
| lluvia | choiva | rain |

### Verb Endings
| Spanish | Galician | Form |
|---------|----------|------|
| hablo | falo | I speak |
| comes | comes | you eat |
| vivimos | vivimos | we live |
| hablan | falan | they speak |
| hablé | falei | I spoke |
| comí | comín | I ate |
