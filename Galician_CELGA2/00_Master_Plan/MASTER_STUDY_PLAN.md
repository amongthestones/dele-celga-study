## Phase 1: Systematic Production (Apr–Aug 2026)

**Goal:** Build accurate Galician output by systematically replacing Spanish interference patterns.

**Priority areas (in order):**
1. Verb endings — present, past, future (differ from Spanish)
2. Article contractions (ao, do, no, polo, etc.)
3. Key vocabulary divergences (noite, leite, moito, choiva, fillo...)
4. Common pronouns and their placement
5. Avoiding Castilianisms in writing

**Activities (see SESSION_GUIDE.md):**
- Saturday: Grammar focus + short writing exercise
- Sunday: Listening (TVG/Radio Galega) + speaking practice

**Milestones:**
- [ ] Complete Grammar Module 1 (Articles & Contractions)
- [ ] Complete Grammar Module 2 (Present Tense)
- [ ] Complete Grammar Module 3 (Past Tenses)
- [ ] Write 10+ short texts (50–100 words each) in Galician
- [ ] Maintain one conversation entirely in Galician (neighbors, shops) per week

---

## Phase 2: Sustained Practice (Sep 2026–Mar 2027)

**Goal:** Produce longer, more accurate texts; hold sustained conversations without switching to Spanish.

**New in Phase 2:**
- Writing texts of 150+ words
- Reading Galician news articles actively (not just passively)
- Formal register practice (for written expression exam task)
- Expand vocabulary beyond the Spanish-Galician interface words

**Milestones:**
- [ ] Write 10+ texts of 150+ words
- [ ] Read 2 Galician articles per week with active annotation
- [ ] Hold a 5-minute sustained conversation without switching to Spanish
- [ ] Study CELGA 2 exam format (see official materials from Xunta de Galicia)

---

## Phase 3: Exam Technique (Apr–Nov 2027)

**Goal:** Know the CELGA 2 format, practice under timed conditions, register and sit the exam.

**CELGA 2 Exam Sections:**
| Section | Duration | What to practice |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| Comprensión oral | ~30 min | TVG/Radio Galega listening tasks |
| Comprensión escrita | ~45 min | Timed reading with comprehension questions |
| Expresión escrita | ~45 min | Short texts in Galician (letter, description) |
| Expresión oral | ~15 min | Individual monologue + interaction |

**Milestones:**
- [ ] Obtain official CELGA 2 preparation materials (Xunta de Galicia)
- [ ] Complete 2+ full mock exams
- [ ] Register for CELGA 2 exam (check dates at lingua.gal)
- [ ] Practice oral tasks: 2-minute monologue on familiar topics
