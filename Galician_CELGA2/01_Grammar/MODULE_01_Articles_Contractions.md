# Module 1: Galician Articles and Contractions
## The Foundation of Galician Grammar

---

## Overview

One of the most distinctive features of Galician is its system of article contractions. Mastering these is essential from day one.

**Time to complete:** 2 weeks
**Prerequisites:** Basic Spanish knowledge

---

## Part 1: Definite Articles

### Basic Forms

| Gender | Singular | Plural |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Masculine | **o** | **os** |
| Feminine | **a** | **as** |

**Examples:**
- o libro (the book)
- a casa (the house)
- os libros (the books)
- as casas (the houses)

### Usage Notes

1. **Before nouns beginning with stressed "a":**
   - Use masculine article if masculine: *o amigo*
   - Feminine keeps *a*: *a auga* (the water)

2. **With proper nouns:**
   - Common with names: *O Xosé chegou* (Xosé arrived)
   - Not used in formal writing for people

---

## Part 2: Indefinite Articles

| Gender | Singular | Plural |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Masculine | **un** | **uns** |
| Feminine | **unha** | **unhas** |

**Examples:**
- un home (a man)
- unha muller (a woman)
- uns nenos (some children)
- unhas cadeiras (some chairs)

**Note:** The feminine "unha" is a key difference from Spanish "una"

---

## Part 3: Article Contractions (Crucial!)

### With Preposition "a" (to, at)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| a + o | **ao** | Vou ao cine (I go to the cinema) |
| a + a | **á** | Vou á escola (I go to school) |
| a + os | **aos** | Falo aos estudantes (I speak to the students) |
| a + as | **ás** | Digo ás nenas (I tell the girls) |

### With Preposition "de" (of, from)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| de + o | **do** | O libro do profesor (the teacher's book) |
| de + a | **da** | A porta da casa (the door of the house) |
| de + os | **dos** | Os fillos dos veciños (the neighbors' children) |
| de + as | **das** | O prezo das mazás (the price of the apples) |

### With Preposition "en" (in, on)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| en + o | **no** | Estou no traballo (I'm at work) |
| en + a | **na** | Está na cociña (S/he's in the kitchen) |
| en + os | **nos** | Penso nos problemas (I think about the problems) |
| en + as | **nas** | Nas rúas de Compostela (In the streets of Compostela) |

### With Preposition "con" (with)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| con + o | **co** | Vou co meu amigo (I go with my friend) |
| con + a | **coa** | Falo coa miña nai (I talk with my mother) |
| con + os | **cos** | Saín cos colegas (I went out with colleagues) |
| con + as | **coas** | Traballo coas mans (I work with my hands) |

### With Preposition "por" (by, through, for)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| por + o | **polo** | Vai polo camiño (Go along the path) |
| por + a | **pola** | Pola mañá (In the morning) |
| por + os | **polos** | Polos seus méritos (For their merits) |
| por + as | **polas** | Polas tardes (In the afternoons) |

### With Preposition "para" (for, to)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| para + o | **para o** or **pro** | É pro neno (It's for the boy) |
| para + a | **para a** or **pra** | Pra muller (For the woman) |
| para + os | **para os** or **pros** | Pros estudantes |
| para + as | **para as** or **pras** | Pras nenas |

*Note: The shorter forms (pro, pra, pros, pras) are common in speech*

### With Preposition "tras" (after, behind)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| tras + o | **tralo** | Tralo xantar (After lunch) |
| tras + a | **trala** | Trala porta (Behind the door) |
| tras + os | **tralos** | Tralos exames (After the exams) |
| tras + as | **tralas** | Tralas vacacións (After the holidays) |

## Part 4: Contractions with Indefinite Articles

### With "de" (of, from)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| de + un | **dun** | Veño dun pobo pequeno |
| de + unha | **dunha** | Fala dunha cousa importante |
| de + uns | **duns** | Sei duns amigos |
| de + unhas | **dunhas** | Coñezo dunhas rapazas |

### With "en" (in)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| en + un | **nun** | Traballo nun banco |
| en + unha | **nunha** | Vivo nunha cidade |
| en + uns | **nuns** | Nuns días volvo |
| en + unhas | **nunhas** | Nunhas horas |

### With "con" (with)

| + Article | = Contraction | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| con + un | **cun** | Saín cun amigo |
| con + unha | **cunha** | Falo cunha veciña |
| con + uns | **cuns** | Cuns poucos euros |
| con + unhas | **cunhas** | Cunhas amigas |

## Part 5: Complete Contraction Chart

| Prep | o | a | os | as | un | unha | uns | unhas |
|------|---|---|----|----|----|----|-----|------|
| **a** | ao | á | aos | ás | - | - | - | - |
| **de** | do | da | dos | das | dun | dunha | duns | dunhas |
| **en** | no | na | nos | nas | nun | nunha | nuns | nunhas |
| **con** | co | coa | cos | coas | cun | cunha | cuns | cunhas |
| **por** | polo | pola | polos | polas | - | - | - | - |
| **tras** | tralo | trala | tralos | tralas | - | - | - | - |

## Part 6: Comparison with Spanish

| Spanish | Galician | English |
|---------|----------|---------|
| al cine | ao cine | to the cinema |
| del libro | do libro | of the book |
| en el coche | no coche | in the car |
| con el niño | co neno | with the child |
| por el camino | polo camiño | along the path |
| en una casa | nunha casa | in a house |
| de una ciudad | dunha cidade | from a city |

**Key differences:**
- Spanish only contracts "a + el = al" and "de + el = del"
- Galician contracts with ALL definite articles
- Galician also contracts with "en", "con", "por", "tras"
- Galician contracts with indefinite articles too

## Common Errors

1. **Forgetting to contract:**
   - ❌ Vou a o cine
   - ✓ Vou ao cine

2. **Using Spanish contractions:**
   - ❌ Vou al cine (Spanish)
   - ✓ Vou ao cine (Galician)

3. **Wrong gender agreement:**
   - ❌ Vou ao escola
   - ✓ Vou á escola (escola is feminine)

4. **Forgetting "en" contractions:**
   - ❌ Estou en a casa
   - ✓ Estou na casa
